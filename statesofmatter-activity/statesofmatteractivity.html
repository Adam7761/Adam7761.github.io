Matter is the content that makes up the universe everything that takes up space and has mass is known as matter. Solids are one of the states of matter. The particles in soilds are packed together. The second states of matter is liquid. The particles in liquids to the shape of the container that they are housed in. Another state of matter is Gas. Gas molecules are either very weak bonds or no bonds at all as a result they can move . Bose-Einstein condensate is a group of atoms cooled to within a hair of absolute zero. When they reach that temperature the atoms are moving. Plasma is a state of matter that the particles move around .
