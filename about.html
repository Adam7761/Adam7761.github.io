About Us


Welcome to Chemistry4You, your number one source for all things Chemistry for year 7. Chemistry4You was Founded in 2020 by Adam Gomma, in response to the COVID 19 Global pandemic resulting in students having to study and work from home. We're dedicated to giving you the very latest information regarding Chemistry, with a strong focus on placing you the student at the centre. We pride ourselves on the fact that out content is accessible to not only the students of all abilites but to all the parents that are supporting their child in learning Chemistry. All the content on the website is clearly explained and defined along with useful videos for the more difficult topics, links to additional information, as well as quizzes that can be completed once you are ready. We hope you enjoy learning on the website as much as we enjoyed providing the content to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
